What kind of pairings work best?

  • Precision
    You crank through code like a well-oiled machine, aerodynamic and a little intense. Your work ethic is appreciated and envied, but please leave the spandex at home.
  • Wet 'n Wild
    Every code assignment is like a crazy slide down a waterfall, but you somehow manage to deliver.
  • Laid Back
    You've got your mind on your money and your money on your mind (saving the gin 'n juice for after work).
  • High Risk
    It's all adrenaline and right on the edge. Will this thing work, or won't it? No worries... the chute usually opens.
  • Catching Air
    The ups and downs are crazy, with a few spills along the way. Similar to Wet 'n Wild, but it's a little rougher when you take a spill. Someone might need to work on their resume.
  • Aggro Noobs
    This is serious business, and it shows on your faces. Take it down one notch and you will live longer.
  • Perfectly Balanced
    On paper, it just doesn't seem like this pairing will work. The tools are a little off and you look a little shaky, however, your balance is impeccable. Everyone thinks you're wacky, but you are the perfect team.
